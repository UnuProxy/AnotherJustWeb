FAQs

WHAT IS INCLUDED IN THE CHARTER PRICE?

Depending on the boat, the offer may change. Welcome drinks, towels and snorkelling equipment are included on all boats.

To view the specific offer for each boat, please click directly on the pictures on the website. Additionally, PDF files can be sent by email or WhatsApp on your request.

IMPORTANT:
The fuel, the captain's fee and the 21% VAT are not included.

HOW DO I BOOK?

Booking your charter with Just Enjoy Ibiza is easy and straightforward:

  1. Contact us through our website, WhatsApp, or email with your preferred date, boat type, and group size
  2. We'll check availability and provide you with confirmation
  3. A deposit (30% of the total amount) is required to secure your booking
  4. Once the deposit is paid, you'll receive a confirmation email with all details

We recommend booking in advance, especially during the high season (June-September), to ensure availability of your preferred boat.

HOW DOES THE PAYMENT WORK?

Our payment process is simple:

  • 30% deposit to secure your booking (via bank transfer or credit card)
  • The remaining 70% can be paid:
    • In cash on the day of the charter
    • By bank transfer (at least 3 days before the charter date)
    • By credit card (subject to an additional 2% fee)

All payments will be confirmed with a receipt. For corporate events or special arrangements, please contact us directly to discuss payment terms.

HOW MUCH IS THE DEPOSIT?

The standard deposit is 30% of the total charter price. This deposit is required to secure your booking and reserve your chosen date and boat.

The deposit is non-refundable but can be used towards a future booking in case of weather cancellations or if you reschedule according to our cancellation policy.

IS FUEL INCLUDED IN THE BOAT PRICE?

No, fuel is not included in the charter price. Fuel is charged separately based on actual consumption.

The fuel consumption varies depending on:

  • The type and size of the boat
  • The distance traveled during your charter
  • Speed and navigation conditions

For planning purposes, we can provide an estimate of fuel costs based on your intended itinerary. On average, fuel costs range from ‚Ç¨100-‚Ç¨400 per day depending on the boat and route.

WHAT HAPPENS IN CASE OF BAD WEATHER ON THE CHARTER DAY?

Your safety is our top priority. In case of adverse weather conditions:

  • If the port authorities issue a weather warning or our captain determines it's unsafe to sail, we will reschedule your charter at no additional cost.
  • We'll work with you to find the next available date that suits your schedule.
  • If rescheduling is not possible during your stay, your deposit can be used for a future booking within one year or refunded according to our cancellation policy.

Weather decisions are typically made 24-48 hours before your charter date. We'll keep you informed about any potential weather issues as your booking date approaches.

WHAT IS THE DURATION OF A CHARTER DAY?

Our standard charter day is 8 hours, typically from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

This timing allows you to:

  • Explore multiple beaches and coves
  • Enjoy lunch at a beach restaurant (if desired)
  • Have plenty of time for swimming, snorkeling, and water activities

Extended hours can be arranged at an additional cost. Half-day charters (4 hours) are also available for some boats, either in the morning (10:00 AM - 2:00 PM) or afternoon (3:00 PM - 7:00 PM).

WHAT IS THE CANCELLATION POLICY

Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • Cancellations made more than 30 days before the charter date: 100% of deposit refundable or transferable to another date
  • Cancellations 15-30 days before the charter date: 50% of deposit refundable or 100% transferable to another date
  • Cancellations less than 15 days before the charter date: deposit is non-refundable but may be transferable to another date at our discretion

For weather-related cancellations determined by the captain or port authorities, we offer 100% rescheduling with no additional fees.

All cancellations must be made in writing via email or WhatsApp.

CAN I BRING MY OWN FOOD AND DRINKS ONTO THE BOAT?

Yes, you are welcome to bring your own food and additional drinks on board.

We provide:

  • Welcome drinks and refreshments (water, soft drinks)
  • Ice and coolers to keep your items cold
  • Basic tableware and glasses

If you prefer, we can also arrange for catering services or pre-order lunch boxes for your day on the water. Please notify us in advance if you plan to bring significant amounts of food or alcoholic beverages so we can ensure proper storage space is available.
